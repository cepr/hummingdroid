HummingDroid - An Android based Quad-Copter

Introduction

HummingDroid is a Quad-Copter using an Android smart-phone as on-board controller. The accelerometer and the gyroscope of the phone are used for attitude determination and control and the radios (WiFi and Mobile data) are used for remote control.

Hardware

The quad copter is composed of:

  • A Nexus 4
  • A Teensy controller to interface the phone to the 4 ESC.
  • A DJI quad-copter frame including the ESC and the motors.
  • A GoPro for recording and FPV
  • A 2.4 GHz video transmitter

Software

The software is available under a GPLv3 license under these GitHub repositories:

  • hummingdroid-flightapp for the on-board Android application.
  • hummingdroid-teensy for the Teensy firmware.
  • hummingdroid-gcs-qt for the Ground application.
